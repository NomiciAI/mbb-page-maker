Component: framework-map
Working rhythm

A five-step rhythm makes ownership visible before work reaches escalation

1Capture

Log the request with required decision context.

2Triage

Apply priority rules and assign the accountable owner.

3Resolve

Handle standard cases within the team boundary.

4Escalate

Move only exceptions with a clear decision ask.

5Learn

Update rules and remove recurring blockers.

Component: process-flow
Scope filter

The pilot should include only work that passes strategic fit, value, and readiness filters

1
Strategic fit

Keep work linked to priority customer journeys or regulatory commitments.

2
Economic value

Prioritize cases with measurable cycle-time, quality, or cost impact.

3
Execution readiness

Confirm owner availability, data access, and operating forum sponsorship.

Pilot scope Two journeys, four teams, one weekly decision forum

The scope is narrow enough to learn quickly and broad enough to test handoffs across functions.

Component: filter-cascade
Roadmap

A staged roadmap creates proof in eight weeks before scaling the operating model

Weeks 1-2Lock design

Confirm pilot journeys, decision owners, intake criteria, and baseline measures.

Weeks 3-4Run pilot

Operate the weekly forum and route live cases through the new decision spine.

Weeks 5-6Refine model

Remove recurring blockers, update templates, and clarify handoff rules.

Weeks 7-8Scale decision

Review evidence, approve broader rollout, and assign wave-two owners.
