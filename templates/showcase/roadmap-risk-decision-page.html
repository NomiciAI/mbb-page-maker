Combination pattern

Pair a phased roadmap with the decisions and controls needed to de-risk execution

0-6 weeksConfirm scope

Select priority workflows, owners, baseline metrics, and release gates.

6-12 weeksPilot controls

Run golden tasks, trace failures, and validate approval thresholds.

3-6 monthsScale platform

Standardize reusable tools, context contracts, and evaluation assets.

6+ monthsInstitutionalize

Move cadence, funding, and performance review into the operating model.

Risk and decision rail

DecisionApprove pilot scope before platform spend.
RiskWorkflow owners may not agree on exception paths.
ControlRequire trace review and rollback for each release.
FallbackIf timing is missing, use `process-flow` instead.
Use the rail for decision friction, not as a second roadmap.
