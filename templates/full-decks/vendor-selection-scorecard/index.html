Requirements

Selection criteria should translate business requirements into a scoring model

1Functional fit

Priority workflows, user roles, and required capabilities.

2Integration fit

Systems, data, APIs, identity, and implementation constraints.

3Commercial fit

Total cost, contract flexibility, scaling economics, and lock-in.

4Risk fit

Security, compliance, resilience, roadmap dependency, and support model.

5Operating fit

Adoption load, admin model, training, and vendor partnership quality.

6Decision fit

The criteria should map directly to approve, pilot, negotiate, or reject.
