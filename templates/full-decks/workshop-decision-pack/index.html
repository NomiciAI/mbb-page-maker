Workshop decision archetype
Session answer

A workshop pack should define what must be decided before it defines the agenda

  • Align on the decision question and success criteria.
  • Review the minimum evidence needed for productive debate.
  • Compare options with a consistent decision lens.
  • Capture decisions, owners, timing, and open evidence.

    • Workshop output

    A useful session ends with changed decisions, not only richer discussion.
