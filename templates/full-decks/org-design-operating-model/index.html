Org design archetype
From-to shifts

Design pages should name the behavioral shifts before drawing boxes

FromShared ownership

Priority work split across functions without a clear decision spine.

ToNamed accountability

One owner drives outcomes, tradeoffs, and escalation.

FromMeeting-led governance

Forums review activity without stable input standards.

ToDecision-led cadence

Forums exist only where they change resource, risk, or scope decisions.

FromCapability gaps

Specialist work depends on a few overloaded roles.

ToReusable capability

Teams use common playbooks, assets, and enablement paths.
