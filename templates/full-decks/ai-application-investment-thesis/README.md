# AI Application Investment Thesis

## Summary

Investment-style full-deck exemplar for evaluating where AI application software value is likely to accrue.

## Visual traits

Dark red cover art, high-density investment memo pacing, wide tables, ranked bars, risk framing, and answer-first slide titles.

## Use when

Use for investment thesis, diligence, product bet, market map, fundraising, or pitch-style decks where evidence supports a clear recommendation.

## Do not use when

Do not use for operating-model transformation, workshop facilitation, or technical architecture decks unless the task is explicitly investment-led.

## Storyline pattern

Cover, recommendation, why now, market evidence, segment comparison, moat logic, risks, and decision close.

## Layout + component stack

Uses cover art, right-rail evidence pages, `metric-strip`, `comparison-table`, `ranked-bars`, `risk-matrix-register`, `decision-log`, and qualitative support components.

## Theme / assets

Theme: `assets/themes/red.css`. Cover asset: `assets/media/covers/ai-application-investment-cover.png`.

## Source inspiration

Source-neutral investment memo, diligence, and category thesis patterns. No external deck, brand system, or consulting-firm identity is copied.

## Evidence shape

Best with market signals, segment comparisons, ranked opportunities, risk records, buyer/job-to-be-done evidence, and decision criteria.
