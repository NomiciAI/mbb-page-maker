# Pricing Commercial Strategy

## Summary

Pricing and commercial strategy archetype for diagnosing revenue leakage, selecting packaging and price moves, and linking actions to sales execution.

## Visual traits

Red commercial action style, pricing architecture table, leakage metrics, customer segment view, scenario sensitivity, risk controls, and execution roadmap.

## Use when

Use for pricing strategy, packaging, discount governance, sales efficiency, revenue uplift, margin improvement, and commercial model redesign.

## Do not use when

Do not use for broad market entry, product strategy, or investor pitch decks unless pricing is the main commercial decision.

## Storyline pattern

Cover, commercial answer, revenue leakage or opportunity, segment willingness, pricing architecture, financial sensitivity, sales/governance model, roadmap and decisions.

## Layout + component stack

Uses `metric-strip`, `comparison-table`, `ranked-bars`, `sensitivity-grid`, `raci-governance-grid`, `phase-roadmap`, and `decision-log`.

## Theme / assets

Theme: `assets/themes/red.css`. No required media assets.

## Source inspiration

Source-neutral commercial strategy and pricing governance patterns. No external deck, brand system, or consulting-firm identity is copied.

## Evidence shape

Best with revenue, margin, discount, customer segment, willingness, packaging, sales motion, scenario, risk, and governance evidence.

## Flexible swaps

Swap sensitivity grid for ranked bars when uplift is directional, replace RACI grid with decision log when governance is unresolved, and replace segment table with heatmap when willingness is scored.

## When to diverge

Diverge when the audience needs a market entry strategy, sales operating plan, or turnaround program rather than a pricing decision.

## Do not copy page order/content

Do not copy the archetype page order, placeholder price moves, footer text, or commercial labels. Use it only to understand pricing-story composition.
