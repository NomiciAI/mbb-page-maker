# Board Decision Recommendation

## Summary

Full-deck archetype for board or executive committee decisions where the audience must approve, fund, stop, gate, or choose among strategic options.

## Visual traits

Dense recommendation style with answer-first pages, option gates, value bridge, risk controls, and explicit decision close.

## Use when

Use for board approval, investment committee, capital authorization, strategic choice, initiative funding, or executive go/no-go decks.

## Do not use when

Do not use for ordinary progress updates, PMO status reads, or workshops where the main job is alignment rather than approval.

## Storyline pattern

Cover, recommendation answer, option score, value and conditions, risks and gates, decision close.

## Layout + component stack

Uses `outcome-support`, `metric-strip`, `checkmark-bar-table`, `waterfall-bridge`, `risk-matrix-register`, `decision-log`, and `phase-meeting-plan`.

## Theme / assets

Theme: `assets/themes/green.css`. Optional generated cover asset: `assets/media/covers/investment-decision-cover.jpg`.

## Source inspiration

Source-neutral board recommendation and investment committee patterns. No external deck, brand system, or consulting-firm identity is copied.

## Evidence shape

Best with options, criteria, value evidence, risks, decision gates, conditions, owners, and funding or approval asks.

## Flexible swaps

Swap `checkmark-bar-table` for `weighted-scorecard` when weights are explicit, swap `waterfall-bridge` for `sensitivity-grid` when assumptions drive the decision, and swap the risk matrix for `decision-log` when governance asks dominate.

## When to diverge

Diverge to `cxo-steerco-progress-update` when the meeting is mainly status, or to `mna-investment-decision` when the decision is a transaction with diligence and valuation depth.

## Do not copy page order/content

Do not copy the archetype page order, placeholder claims, footer text, or decision labels. Use it only to understand board-decision pacing and component combinations.
