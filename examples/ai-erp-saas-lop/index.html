Product thesis

The winning AI product is a decision co-pilot layer embedded in ERP workflow, not a feature bundle

Product thesis Decision co-pilot layer

Sense workflow state, recommend the next action, explain confidence, and execute only inside permissioned guardrails.

Read the workflow Use tenant context, role, process state, open exceptions, and policy to understand the user's current decision.
  • Detect task state, blockers, approver path, and exception severity.
  • Retrieve only permissioned records, configuration, and policy context.
  • Package the current decision into a reusable workflow-state object.
Recommend and act with evidence Explain the proposed action, source trace, confidence, business impact, and required approval class.
  • Rank next actions by value, risk, and policy fit.
  • Show source trail, confidence band, and expected operating impact.
  • Execute draft, recommend, or route-for-approval actions by guardrail.
Learn from overrides Turn edits, rejections, escalations, and outcomes into reusable product telemetry and evaluation assets.
  • Capture user edits, rejection reasons, and downstream business outcomes.
  • Promote repeated patterns into tests, prompts, and decision policies.
  • Feed product roadmap with adoption friction and margin-improvement signals.
